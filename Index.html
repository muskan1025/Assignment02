Emojis and their meaning-

😍

Smiling Face with Heart-Shaped Eyes

The emoji with a smiling face and two heart-shaped eyes typically conveys a strong sense of happiness, love, or affection. It's often used to express extreme joy, admiration, or being enamored with something or someone.

😎

Face with Pleading Eyes

In this emoji, wearing sunglasses makes us look cool and effortless, and this emoji is used to capture that feeling: someone or something that is totally awesome.

💕

Two Hearts

The “two hearts” emoji meaning is pretty simple. One heart is you, and the other heart is someone you love.

🤦

Face Palm

Thats right, this handy emoji is meant to indicate embarrassment or frustration, with yourself and others.
